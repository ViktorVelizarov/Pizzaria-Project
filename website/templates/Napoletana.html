Napoletana Pizza

Ingredients:

  • San Marzano Tomatoes
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • Fresh Basil Leaves
  • Garlic

Allergens:

This Napoletana pizza may contain the following allergens:

  • Milk (Mozzarella Cheese)

The Napoletana pizza is a classic Neapolitan pizza known for its simplicity and traditional ingredients. It features San Marzano tomatoes, fresh mozzarella cheese, extra virgin olive oil, fresh basil leaves, and garlic.
