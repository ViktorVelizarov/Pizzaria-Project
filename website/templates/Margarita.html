Margarita Pizza

Ingredients:

  • Tomato Sauce
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Fresh Basil Leaves
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper

Allergens:

This Margarita pizza may contain the following allergens:

  • Milk (Mozzarella Cheese)

The Margarita pizza is a classic Italian pizza known for its simplicity and delicious flavors. It features a tomato sauce base, topped with fresh mozzarella cheese and basil leaves, drizzled with olive oil, and seasoned with salt and black pepper.
