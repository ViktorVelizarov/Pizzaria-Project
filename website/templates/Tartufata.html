Tartufata Pizza

Ingredients:

  • Mushrooms
  • Truffle Cream
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Fresh Basil Leaves
  • Olive Oil

Allergens:

This Tartufata pizza may contain the following allergens:

  • Milk (Mozzarella Cheese)

The Tartufata pizza is a gourmet delight, featuring a combination of mushrooms, truffle cream, mozzarella cheese, fresh basil leaves, and a drizzle of olive oil for a rich and earthy flavor.
