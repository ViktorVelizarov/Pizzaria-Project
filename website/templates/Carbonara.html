Carbonara Pizza

Ingredients:

  • Creamy Carbonara Sauce
  • Pancetta or Bacon
  • Grated Pecorino Romano Cheese
  • Black Pepper
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Olive Oil

Allergens:

This Carbonara pizza may contain the following allergens:

  • Milk (Creamy Carbonara Sauce, Mozzarella Cheese)
  • Pork (Pancetta or Bacon)

The Carbonara pizza is a delightful Italian creation that brings the flavors of the classic pasta dish to a pizza. It features a creamy carbonara sauce, pancetta or bacon, grated Pecorino Romano cheese, and a touch of black pepper. All of this is complemented by mozzarella cheese and a drizzle of olive oil.
